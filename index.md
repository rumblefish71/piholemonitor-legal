## 💡 Our Core Principles / Unsere Prinzipien

> **Privacy by Design:** We do not track your browsing habits. Your data belongs to you.  
> **No Middleman:** Communication happens directly between your device and your Pi-hole®.  
> **Transparency:** Crash reports are strictly opt-in. You decide if you want to help us improve.
